Simmate

The Simulated Materials Ecosystem (Simmate) is a toolbox and helper for materials science research. It lets you explore various crystal databases, predict new materials, and quickly calculate properties (electronic, elastic, thermodynamic, and more).

Created at the University of North Carolina by Jack Sundberg, Simmate's ulitmate goal is to make materials science approachable for beginners, while enabling experts to work efficiently. All software is free and open-source. If you'd like to download Simmate's framework and use it yourself, take a look at our github page.
