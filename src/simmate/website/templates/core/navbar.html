About

Created at the University of North Carolina (Chapel Hill) by Prof. Scott Warren and Jack Sundberg, Putty Exams' ulitmate goal is to create a fair and cheater-free enviornment for students and instructors. Exams are made to be flexible and ultimately unique to each individual student, which is where we get our website name Putty Exams from! Even as exam questions mold uniquely for each student, they still retain the same context.
